Why Common Ground?

The future of technology is no longer built in silos. AI engineers, developers, product thinkers, designers, founders, researchers, and students are shaping the same ecosystem. Common Ground brings these worlds into one room.

Hosted at the Microsoft Office in Hyderabad, it is a curated gathering of people building what's next. Keynotes, technical deep dives, career conversations, and meaningful networking create opportunities to learn from industry leaders and connect with a community driven by curiosity and innovation.

This is where ideas become collaborations, conversations become opportunities, and individuals become part of something larger than themselves.

Learn. Connect. Build. Together.

FAQ

Who can attend? +

Students and professionals from all technology and design disciplines are welcome.

Is registration mandatory? +

Yes. You must apply in advance, but submitting the form does not confirm a seat. Only applicants who receive a separate confirmation email will be admitted.

Does submitting the form confirm my seat? +

No. Your submission is an application. Capacity is limited, applications are reviewed, and entry is granted only through an official confirmation email.

Will certificates be provided? +

Yes. All verified attendees will receive a digital certificate of participation.

Is prior experience required? +

No. Sessions cater to a wide range of experience levels — from beginners to seasoned professionals.

When will the detailed agenda be released? +

A program preview is available above. Final speaker timings and session details will be shared closer to the event, including with confirmed attendees by email.
